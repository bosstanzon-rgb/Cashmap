# CashMap Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Who we are
CashMap is an independent tool for gig drivers. We are not affiliated with Uber Eats, Mr D, Bolt, or any delivery platform.

## What data we collect

### Location data (opt-in only)
When you enable "Share anonymous heatmap data" in Settings AND tap "I'm Working":
- Your GPS coordinates are sent to our server
- Coordinates are stored at ~1km grid precision (rounded) for community heatmaps
- We **never** store your exact GPS coordinates in a way that is readable by other users
- Your location data is automatically deleted after 7 days

### Mileage logs (personal, never shared)
When you track a shift:
- Distance driven (km) is stored against an anonymous device identifier
- **Only you can read your own mileage logs** — no other user or admin can access them
- Used only for your personal tax export
- Automatically deleted after 365 days

### Shift logs (personal + anonymised community)
When you log earnings after a shift:
- Your earnings, suburb, and platform are stored against an anonymous device identifier
- **Only you can read your personal shift logs**
- An anonymised version (suburb + platform + earnings amount, no identifier) contributes to community predictions
- This helps all drivers get better zone predictions

### What we do NOT collect
- Your name, email, or phone number
- Your exact home or work address
- Payment information
- Device identifiers that can identify you personally

## Your anonymous identifier
CashMap generates a random ID (e.g. `sess_abc123xyz`) stored only on your device. This is used to scope your personal data. It is **not** linked to your identity. If you reset the app, a new ID is generated and your old data becomes permanently inaccessible.

## Data sharing
- **Community heatmap**: anonymised location pings (rounded to ~1km, auto-deleted after 7 days)
- **Community predictions**: suburb + platform + earnings (no identifier)
- We **never sell** your data to any third party
- We **never share** your personal mileage or shift data with anyone

## Your rights (POPIA / GDPR)
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Reset the app in Settings to view your session ID
- **Delete**: Use "Reset CashMap" in Settings to delete all local data. Email support to request server-side deletion
- **Opt out**: Turn off "Share anonymous heatmap data" in Settings at any time

## Data retention
| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Location pings | 7 days |
| Mileage logs | 365 days |
| Shift logs | Until you reset |
| Predictions cache | Local only, 15 min |

## Contact
For data deletion requests or privacy concerns: support@cashmap.app

## Legal basis
- **Legitimate interest**: Anonymous community heatmap data helps all drivers earn more
- **Consent**: You explicitly opt in to location sharing
- **Contract**: Mileage and shift logs are stored to provide the service you requested
